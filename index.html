SleepSync Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

Last updated: July 5, 2026

SleepSync ("we," "our," or "us") is a sleep tracker with scoring, insights, and sleep-debt analysis for iOS, developed by SudarshanTechLabs. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app does and does not collect, how it is used, and the choices you have. By installing or using SleepSync you agree to the practices described below.

Overview

SleepSync is designed to be private by default. Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate accounts, servers, or databases that hold your personal information, and we cannot see the content you create in the app.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require an account or ask for your name, email, or phone number.
  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with anyone.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers or track you across apps or websites.

Data You Create

Any content you enter into SleepSync is stored locally on your device using Apple’s standard on-device storage. It stays under your control. Deleting the app removes this local data. If you have iCloud backup enabled for the device, your data may be included in your own encrypted Apple backup, which is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Permissions

Notifications

Used to deliver local reminders and alerts you schedule inside the app. Notifications are generated on your device; no notification content is sent to us or any server. You can turn them off at any time in iOS Settings → SleepSync → Notifications.

Third-Party Services

SleepSync does not use advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or any third-party tracking services. The app makes no network requests that share your personal data.

Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored on your device and protected by iOS app sandboxing and the device security controls you enable (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). Because your data lives on your device, keeping your device secure is the best way to protect it.

Children’s Privacy

SleepSync is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date above.
